About Me

I'm an experienced Software Engineer with a proven track record in driving revenue and operational efficiency. I currently work at Citigroup, where I implement revenue-boosting features and have achieved substantial cost reductions through process automation.

My journey has taken me from my home country of Venezuela, where I was born and raised, to the United States approximately seven years ago. Early on in my career, I harbored a fervent desire to expand my knowledge in computer science, a field not readily accessible in my homeland. Upon arriving in the U.S., I discovered the exciting realm of computer science and began delving into various domains, including full-stack development, game development, and mobile development. These pursuits not only served as a source of entertainment but also offered valuable learning experiences throughout the journey.

My educational background also includes a role as a Full-Stack developer at the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill. Here, I made significant contributions by enhancing operations and crafting impactful web solutions for esteemed departments like the Biophysics and Biochemistry departments. Furthermore, notable projects such as Melophyle and the MiniJava Compiler highlight my expertise in languages like Java, Python, and more.

With fluency in English and Spanish, as well as intermediate proficiency in Italian, I bring both technical prowess and linguistic versatility. Beyond my professional accomplishments, I'm an avid pool player, a movie enthusiast, and a passionate traveler.
